ABOUT US

"We don't just build a business, we build relations"

Shah Group of companies has been in the textile industry since 1971. With more than 45 years of experience, Shah Group has evolved into a one stop shop for all kinds of yarn sourcing. We today cater to almost all major domestic markets in India including centres like Bhiwandi, Malegaon, Ichalkaranji etc. Our clientele ranges from individual weavers to major corporate entities. Since Inception, our most important objective is our commitment to provide the best distribution network for our partner spinning mills. We thrive to achieve excellence and success through constantly expanding our yarn portfolio and our distribution network nationally and internationally.
